About Me

I am a composition and computer science student at Southern Methodist University (SMU) with an interest in working in videogame music and sound. Through collaborations between SMU's composition department and the SMU Guildhall videogame graduate school, I've had the chance to compose for 3 games and direct the composition team for 2 games. I've always loved listening to game music, both in the game itself and as a standalone piece, so working on the music projects—and holding leadership positions for these—at SMU has been an incredible opportunity. In addition to composing, I love coding, and have worked with various sound packages using DSP and FFT.

In essence, I'm a nerd for the artistic and technical aspects of sound with a knack for project delivery and management. I'm excited to explore the intersections of sound and technology even further.
