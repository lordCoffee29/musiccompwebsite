Mirorred Phantoms

Mirrored Phantoms

February 24th, 2025

Prepare to be consumed by Mirrored Phantoms, a chilling descent into a decaying mannequin factory where reality itself has fractured. This first-person psychological horror experience preys on your deepest fears, immersing you in an unsettling atmosphere where the line between what is real and what is imagined blurs with every haunting step on your journey to escape.

The decaying walls whisper secrets of a dark past, while the lifeless eyes of countless mannequins follow your every move. Explore the oppressive corridors, piecing together the mystery of the shattered mirror shards that hold the key to your escape. But be warned, the silence is deceptive. There are things that lurk in these halls that never want you to leave, and they are always listening.

Steel your nerves for chilling encounters that will test your sanity. Uncover the sinister history of the factory, decipher the clues etched into its decaying walls, and confront the phantoms that haunt your every waking moment. Dare to escape the clutches of this nightmarish reality, or risk being forever trapped within its mirrored depths.

Music

I was the director for the music composition teams and led the team composing for Mirrored Phantoms. This soundtrack is unique in that it's built in layers rather than individual, standalone tracks. It's meant to slowly build up a spooky and tense atmosphere that has the player on their toes at all times.

I composed and mixed tracks for exploration layers, particularly for the second and fourth sections of the game. Below are sample "flat" environments of the layers interacting with each other. I also wrote sound effects/stingers for the mannequins in the game, a few of which are below as well. These are from sounds gathered from recordings of bowed percussion (prominently featuring crotales, vibraphone, marimba, and thunder sheet).
