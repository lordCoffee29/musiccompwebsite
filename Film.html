Film


At SMU, I've had the opportunity to score two films and produce a film around a piece I composed.

MVP

MVP is about an ex-football player with severe CTE attempting to reconnect with his family at his son's graduation. The score conveys intensity and angst with hints of sadness and despair.

Housewarming

Housewarming is a horror film where a college student moving into a new apartment takes an unexpected twist. This score contains elements of foreshadowing that lead to an explosive finale.

Prisoner of the Strings

Prisoner of the Strings is a dance music video that portrays the damaging effects of censorship and book bans through the imagery of a puppet. I won a grant from the Engaged Learning Fellowship to put a team of musicians, filmmakers, and a dancer together and produce this project.
