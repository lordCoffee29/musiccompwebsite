Fastival

Fastival

June 14th, 2024

Fastival is an arcade racing game where players speed through different attractions within a vibrant and cartoony carnival throughout each race. Knock down targets as you race to trigger unique effects on the track that you can use to your advantage. Collect wacky items that can be used to mess with other racers to enhance your driving capabilities.

Music

I led a team of composers to create the tracks for this game—one main menu theme, three race tracks (each with three levels of progression), and fanfare effects. The theme of the game centered around a carnival, with jazz influences. Each level features a clarinet on lap 1, a saxophone on lap 2, and a trumpet in a higher key on lap 3.

I composed and mixed the lap tracks for Level 3 and co-composed and mixed the lap tracks and some alternate mid-section options for Level 2.
